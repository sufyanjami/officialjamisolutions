    About Us

    Jami Solutions is a Software Agency located in Calgary, AB Canada that provides website and marketing solutions for businesses and individuals using AI. Whether you’re a small organization or a large organization, we have a solution package tailored to serve your needs.

    Our Mission

    Our vision is to serve every country with internet access on the planet through software. We love what we do and we want to serve others with our passion while helping them reach their business goals with integrity and team work.

    Our Plan

    We get a detailed understanding of the client we are serving and view them as our own business. Your success is our success so we take this very seriously when it comes to execution of business needs.

    Our Vision

    Our shared vision is to serve a billion people through software. Wether that’s through our clients or our agency, we believe we will make a world a better place through software.
